My Decisions

Every action you fire now carries a lifecycle past “synced”. This is where the loop closes — open commitments, what was predicted, and whether it actually worked. The outcomes feed back into model calibration below.
Akashic used to be fire-and-forget: an action synced to systems and was forgotten. Now each decision is tracked to its realized outcome, auto-schedules a follow-up, and writes its result back into the confidence models — so the system learns from its own track record.
